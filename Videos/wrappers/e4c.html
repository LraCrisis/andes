Viewing Tips
  • Videos play in  Microsoft's Windows Media Player
  • Videos are best viewed in full-screen mode. When Media Player is active:
        To Enter full-screen mode: hold Alt and press Enter
        To Escape from full-screen mode: type Esc
  • Make sure sound is on and volume is up.
  • Screen resolution of 1024 x 768 or higher is required for adequate appearance.
