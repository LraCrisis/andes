Troubleshooting Tips:

  • Clicking a video link should launch Windows Media Player. If Media Player gives error messages, try the following workaround:
    1. Right-click on the video link to get a pop-up menu.
    2. Select Save Target As (Internet Explorer) or Save Link As (Firefox) to download the .wmv video file to your computer
    3. Choose to save the .wmv video file on your desktop
    4. Double-click the .wmv video file you just downloaded to play it.
  • Firefox users: Install the Windows Media Plugin for Firefox or else use the workaround above.
  • Media Player reports missing codec? Install the ACELP.net audio codec.

For best viewing:

  • Make sure your sound is on, your speakers/headphones are plugged in, and your system/speaker volume controls are set appropriately.
  • Enlarge the Media Player window until you get a good appearance, either by clicking and dragging on the window border or by using the maximize button located in the upper right-hand corner (second from the right). You may need to put the player into full-screen mode to get an adequate viewing size:
    • To enter full screen mode:  hold down the Alt key and press Enter.
    • To exit full-screen mode:  type Alt-Enter again or by hitting ESC.
    You can also switch in or out of full-screen mode by double-clicking on the video image or by right-clicking on the video image and selecting "Full Screen" or "Exit Full Screen" from the popup menu.
  • A screen resolution of 1024 x 768 or higher is required for adequate appearance.
