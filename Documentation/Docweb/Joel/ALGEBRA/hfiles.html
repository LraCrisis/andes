Header files used for the Andes2 algebra system

.h files

dbg.hdefines symbolically the bit flags for turning on diagnostics
decl.hdeclares most of the functions, other than local ones.
dimens.hdefines the dimens class
expr.hdefines the expr class and its subclasses, and physvar and oper
extoper.hdefines the constant instantiations of oper
extstruct.h   defines the most important global objects.
indyset.hdefines the indyset class
indysgg.hdeclares the functions of indysgg
lrdcstd.hsome of Linwood's definitions
mconst.hdefines pi, e, ln10, and others
pconsts.hdefines physical constants.
prefixes.hgives the allowable prefixes for units
Solver.hdeclares the functions of Solver.cpp
standard.hmore Linwood defines
unitabr.hdefines the unitabrs class
units.hgives allowable unit abbreviations, their values, and whether they can take prefixes or not
valander.hdefines the valander class, which is mostly the gradient
