Early discussion of need for Units in Andes

On August 25, 2000, I sent an note introducing myself to Bob Shelby. Here is an extract from that message:

... I did wonder about a few points which I find objectionable, and wonder how the physicists actually using this stuff feel about it.

1) Andes completely ignores units. Most instructors of introductory physics I know make a big point of requiring units in answers, but even more importantly, in using units to check on the validity of equations written down. Not to mention using dimensional analysis.
