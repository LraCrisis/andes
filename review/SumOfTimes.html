Suppose a problem has three time points, T0, T1 and T2.  Then the duration of T0 to T2 is equation to the sum of the duration of T0 to T1 and the duration of T1 to T2.  That is, if the duration variables are T02, T01 and T12, then T02=T01+T12.

If you are seeking one of these durations, then you can select "Sum of times" if you want to use this equation to find it.
